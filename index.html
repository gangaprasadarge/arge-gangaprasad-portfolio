SalesInsight360-Regional-Product-Performance-Dashboard

This project involved developing a robust Sales Analysis Dashboard using a combination of SQL for data extraction and transformation, and Power BI for interactive visualization. The dashboard tracks critical Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) such as total revenue, sales by region, and top-performing products. It features dynamic filtering capabilities, allowing users to drill down into data by region, product category, and time period for in-depth analysis. The visualizations, including line charts for trends, pie charts for distribution, and stacked bar charts for comparisons, provide quick and intuitive insights into sales patterns, seasonal variations, and overall business performance.
